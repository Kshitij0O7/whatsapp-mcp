# Policies — Data, Uploads, Account

> Only the items stated below are confirmed. For anything not covered here — especially detailed
> security, privacy, legal, or data-handling questions — **do not fabricate a policy**. Acknowledge
> the question and escalate (category `KB_GAP` or `LEGAL`) so the team can respond accurately.

## Uploads
- There are currently **no upload limits** imposed by SolarTechy.
- Users can upload the technical documents needed for project design (e.g. **solar panel** and
  **inverter specifications**), which the platform uses for automatic calculations.

## Account & data
- Each user registers with login details plus basic personal and company information.
- Users can manage their account and company details from the **Profile** section.

The platform is **free during early access**, so there are no billing or refund specifics defined.
Any billing, invoice, GST, or refund question should be **escalated** (see `escalation.md`),
not answered with invented details.

## How the assistant should handle policy questions
- Confirmed items above → answer directly.
- Anything else (security depth, privacy, legal, data deletion, contracts) → **escalate**, and tell
