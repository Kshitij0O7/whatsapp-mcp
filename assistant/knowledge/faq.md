# FAQ — Core Questions

These are ~25 **core** FAQs. The assistant should generalize from them to answer close variations
(e.g. "Can I export a PDF?" also covers "How do I download my proposal?"). If a question falls
outside these and the other knowledge files, **do not guess** — escalate (see `escalation.md`).

---

### General

**What is SolarTechy?**
An online platform that helps solar businesses create 3D rooftop solar proposals, quotations, and
CAD-like panel layouts in minutes. See `company.md`.

**Who is it for?**
Solar installers, EPC companies, and solar consultants/professionals.

**Is SolarTechy free?**
Yes — it's currently **free for all early users** during early access. See `pricing.md`.

**Is there a trial?**
Yes — click **Start Free Trial** on https://solartechy.in. During early access the full platform is
free.

**Is it online? Do I need to install anything?**
It's fully **online / browser-based** — no installation. Best on Google Chrome on desktop/laptop.

**Who founded SolarTechy?**
It was founded by **Atharva** (ex-IITian).

In the **Profile** section of the dashboard.

### Solar design

**Can I design residential / commercial / industrial projects?**
Yes — all three are supported.

**Is 3D design available?**
Yes — SolarTechy creates 3D rooftop designs, for flat and sloped roofs.

**Can I change tilt / orientation, and place panels manually or automatically?**
Yes — you can adjust tilt and orientation, place panels manually, or let the platform place them
automatically.

**Do you support CAD / manual precision placement?**
Yes — **Precision Design Mode** gives AutoCAD-like precision (walkways, snapping, alignment, TAB
shortcut) with no AutoCAD knowledge needed. See Tutorial 3.

---

### Engineering & calculations

**Can it size inverters / calculate strings / DC & AC capacity?**
Yes — it handles inverter sizing, string calculations, and DC/AC capacity.

**Can it estimate generation and savings?**
Yes — it estimates energy generation and savings.

**Does it do cable sizing, voltage drop, or shadow analysis?**
Yes — it calculates cable size and voltage drop and performs shadow/shading analysis, plus panel
placement optimization.

Yes — professional quotations and proposals, in about 2 minutes end-to-end.

Yes — you can export to PDF and download, print, and share reports.

Yes — you can add your logo, company details, and customer details, and customize the quotation.

### Devices & support

**Can I use it on mobile / tablet / iPad?**
Not currently — mobile and tablet aren't supported yet. Use **Google Chrome on a desktop or
laptop**. See `support.md`.

**Which browser should I use?**
**Google Chrome** (latest version).

**How do I contact support / how do I book a demo?**
For a demo or onboarding session, I can set that up — I'll just need a few details and the team will
reach out (see `demo.md`). For direct contact, see `contact.md`.

### Business & CRM

**Can I manage leads and tasks / assign work to my team?**
Yes — the platform includes **Leads**, **Tasks** (assign work to team members), and **Documents**
(upload panel/inverter specs for automatic calculations) — a lightweight CRM.
