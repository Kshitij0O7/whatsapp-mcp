# Tutorials

SolarTechy has a short tutorial series that helps users get started quickly.

- 📚 Tutorials page: **https://solartechy.in/tutorials**

Share the specific video that matches what the customer is trying to do.

---

## Tutorial 1 — Introduction to SolarTechy
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RnM2OlHozTk

Overview of the platform: the dashboard, CRM, lead management, document management, and the
proposal workflow. Explains how to register, log in, and navigate the platform.

**Recommend when the customer asks about:**
- What is SolarTechy? / How do I get started? / How do I register?
- Dashboard overview
- CRM, Leads, Tasks, Documents

---

## Tutorial 2 — Create Your First 3D Solar Proposal
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3yT3Lt6dMh0

Walks through creating your first rooftop solar project and generating a proposal.

**Recommend when the customer asks about:**
- How do I create my first project / first design?
- Rooftop design
- Proposal generation

---

## Tutorial 3 — Precision Design Mode
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lh3HXcbpe80

Learn how to manually place solar panels with **AutoCAD-like precision without any AutoCAD
knowledge** — including walkways, the TAB shortcut, snapping, and alignment.

**Recommend when the customer asks about:**
- Manual panel placement / Precision Design
- AutoCAD-like design
- Walkways, snapping, alignment
- TAB shortcut

## Onboarding sessions
Beyond the videos, SolarTechy runs **free onboarding sessions** where the team personally helps
users create their first solar design/project and answers questions. If a customer wants an
onboarding session or live demo, collect their details and escalate (see `demo.md`).
