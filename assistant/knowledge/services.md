# Services & Features

SolarTechy is an all-in-one platform for designing solar systems, generating proposals and
quotations, and managing the sales pipeline. Everything below is available inside the web app.

## Core workflow (≈ 2 minutes)
1. Create a **3D rooftop solar design**
2. The platform runs **automatic calculations and pricing**
3. Generate a **professional quotation / proposal**
4. Send it to the customer

## Solar design
- Design **residential**, **commercial**, and **industrial** projects
- **3D rooftop design**
- Works for **flat roofs** and **sloped roofs**
- Change **panel orientation** and **tilt**
- **Place panels manually**, or let the platform **place panels automatically**

## Precision Design Mode
- Manually place solar panels with **AutoCAD-like precision — without any AutoCAD knowledge**
- Supports **walkways**, **snapping**, and **alignment**
- Keyboard helpers such as the **TAB shortcut**
- (See Tutorial 3 in `tutorials.md`.)

## Engineering & calculations
- **Inverter sizing**
- **String calculations**
- **DC capacity** and **AC capacity**
- **Generation estimate** (expected energy output)
- **Savings estimate**
- **Cable size** and **voltage drop**
- **Shadow / shading analysis**
- **Panel placement optimization**

## Proposals & reports
- Generate **quotations** and **proposals**
- **Export to PDF**
- **Customize** the quotation
- Add your **company logo** and **company details**
- Add **customer details**
- **Download**, **print**, and **share** reports

## Business / CRM tools
- **Leads** — track prospective customers
- **Tasks** — assign work to team members (lightweight CRM)
- **Documents** — upload solar **panel** and **inverter specifications**; these are used for
  automatic calculations during design and quotation
- **Profile** — manage account and company details
- **Dashboard** — company growth statistics

## Good to know
- The platform is **online / browser-based** — no software installation required.
- Best experience on **Google Chrome**, on **desktop or laptop** (see `support.md`).
- Uploading panel/inverter specs improves calculation accuracy and reduces manual work.
