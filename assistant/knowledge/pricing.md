# Pricing

## Current status
SolarTechy is currently **FREE for all early users**. This is a **limited-time early-access offer**
before the official launch.

## What to tell customers
- "The platform is **currently free for early users**. If pricing changes in the future,
  we'll announce it on our website."
- There is **free access to the full platform** during early access.
- Bonus mentioned in outreach: users who join now and **close a client through the platform**
  receive continued free access to the program.

## Hard rules (must follow)
- **Never invent, estimate, or promise future prices**, plans, discounts, or amounts.
- **Never** quote a specific figure, currency amount, subscription term, or GST detail —
  none have been defined.
- If a customer pushes for future pricing, plan tiers, enterprise pricing, or a custom quote:
  - Give the "currently free / future changes announced on website" line, **and**
  - If they want a specific commercial arrangement (enterprise, custom licensing, invoicing),
    treat it as an **escalation** (see `escalation.md`) so a human can handle it.

## Payments / invoices / refunds
No public payment, invoicing, or refund details have been defined (the product is free during
early access). Any question about **payment methods, invoices, GST, charges, or refunds** should be
**escalated** to the team rather than answered with specifics.
