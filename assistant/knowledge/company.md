# Company — SolarTechy

## What SolarTechy is
SolarTechy is an online platform that helps solar businesses **increase their sales for free**
by dramatically speeding up the proposal and quotation process. Instead of creating proposals
manually, users generate professional **3D rooftop solar proposals** and **customer quotations**
automatically — the full workflow from design to quotation takes about **2 minutes**.

## Who it's for
- Solar installers
- EPC companies
- Solar consultants and professionals

## Why it matters
Customers expect fast responses. Being able to send a professional proposal within minutes helps
solar businesses build trust, look more capable, and close more deals.

## What you can do with SolarTechy
- 🏠 Create detailed, interactive **3D rooftop solar proposals**
- 📊 Get **complete calculations and pricing** instantly
- 📄 Generate **professional quotations**
- 🛠️ Design solar panel layouts with an easy **CAD-like interface — no AutoCAD knowledge required**
- 📋 Manage **leads, tasks, and project documents** from one platform

(For the full feature breakdown, see `services.md`.)

## Availability
- **Online / web-based** platform — nothing to install.
- Best used on **Google Chrome** on a **desktop or laptop** (see `support.md` for device support).

## Getting started
1. Visit **https://solartechy.in**
2. Click **Start Free Trial**
3. Register with your login details, basic personal information, and company information
4. You'll land on your dashboard

### Dashboard sections
- **Dashboard/overview** — company growth statistics
- **Leads** — track prospective customers through the sales process
- **Tasks** — assign work to team members (lightweight CRM)
- **Documents** — upload solar panel and inverter specifications, used for automatic calculations
- **Profile** — manage your account and company profile
